Fresh Sense

The New Way to Protect Mushroom Farms Using the Power of Smell

What is FreshSense?

FreshSense is a simple, easy-to-use system that “smells” the air inside mushroom farms. Imagine a small device, about the size of your hand, quietlyworking in the corner of a grow room. It reads the air every second, looking forunusual odors that may signal problems such as mold, bad bacteria, or issueswith the growing material.Once FreshSense detects something unusual, it sends a quick alert to thefarmer’s phone. This means the farmer can take action right away—long beforethe problem becomes visible.FreshSense acts like a “nose that never sleeps,” helping farmers protect theirharvest 24 hours a day.

Why FreshSense is Important

Prevents crop loss

A single contamination can destroy 30-70% of the harvest. FreshSense alerts farmers early, giving them time to fix the issue and save their crops.

Saves money

Losing a harvest is expensive. FreshSense protects farms from costly damage by keeping the environment stable and safe.

Reduces stress

Farmers no longer need to constantly check and worry. FreshSense watches over the farm 24/7.

Improves mushroom quality

Healthy mushrooms grow better, look better, and sell at a higher price.

Helps small and big farms

FreshSense works for backyard growers, mid-sized farms, and large commercial operations.
